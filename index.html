Problem to Solution
From Problem to MVP Solution
Building a targeted product to address core assessment bottlenecks
1. Current Problem
• Manual Grading Overhead Evaluating handwritten math and subjective exams manually consumes massive off-duty hours.
• Fragmented Assessment Setup Creating rubrics, exams, and grading keys is scattered across disconnected offline documents.
• Cohort Management Complexity Educators struggle to coordinate multiple classrooms, students, and exam variants simultaneously.
⚠️ Process Bottlenecks
2. Our MVP Solution
• Integrated Exam & Rubric Maker Construct digital structures and marking rubrics directly inside the platform.
• Centralized Classroom Dashboard Organize classes, track students, and manage assignment batches in one place.
• AI-Assisted Assessment Automate paper evaluations based on student responses while keeping teachers in full control of final scores.
✨ Smart Automation
3. Why This MVP Matters
• Time Recovery Slashes grading turnaround time, letting teachers focus on proactive student remediation.
• Standardized Accuracy Removes grading biases and calculation mistakes to ensure rubric consistency.
• Infinite Scalability Empowers individual tutors, tutoring centers, and schools to expand cohort sizes effortlessly.
